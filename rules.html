Player Eligibility

  • A player may only be registered in one age group.
  • No player may play in both the U/13 and U/15 sections.
  • A player may only play for one team throughout the tournament.
  • Copies of birth certificates with ID photos or player cards must always be available.
  • Failure to provide identification in the event of an age query will result in team disqualification.

Match Duration

  • U/13: 20 minutes per half.
  • U/15: 25 minutes per half.
  • Halftime break: 5 minutes.
  • Teams must switch sides within 5 minutes of halftime.
  • No extra time or added time beyond the allocated match duration.

Match Regulations

  • Rolling substitutes are allowed.
  • Teams arriving more than 5 minutes late will forfeit the match.
  • The opposition will be awarded a 2-0 victory in case of a forfeit.

Knockout Stage Rules

  • Penalties will be used to determine the winner in knockout matches.
  • Each team will take 5 penalties.
  • If still tied after 5 penalties, sudden death will apply.

Medical Assistance

  • A medic will be present at each field to attend to injuries.
  • An ambulance will be available on standby for emergencies.

Complaints Procedure

  • Teams will be allowed 20 minutes after the final whistle to lodge any complaints or concerns regarding the match.
  • This can be done by speaking with the designated tournament staff or organisers.

Match Report Cards

  • Please note that once a coach signs the match report card, it is considered final and no further complaints will be reviewed.
  • Therefore, please ensure that you carefully review the match report card before signing it.
